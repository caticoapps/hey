Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. We aim for full transparency on how we collect, store, use, and share your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our mobile application and/or any related services or websites (we refer to them collectively as the "Services"). It also describes how you can control and protect your privacy. If there are any terms in this Privacy Policy that you do not agree with, please discontinue use of our Services.

Information We Collect

We are thoughtful about the personal information we ask you to provide and the personal information that we collect about you through the operation of our Services. We only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so, for example, to provide our Services, to communicate with you, or to make our Services better.

Information You Provide to Us

  • Account data: We ask for basic information from you in order to set up your account and provide our Services. We require individuals who sign up for an account to provide an email address and date of birth to verify you fulfill the minimum age requirement to use our Services. Your date of birth stays private, only your age is shown in your public profile.
  • Profile data: If you have an account with us, we collect the information that you provide for your public profile, namely your username, gender and profile photo. This information is required to use our Services, however it is up to you whether you disclose your gender, your real name and real photo of your face. You can provide any information you want unless it does not breach our Terms of Service.
  • Communications data: You may also provide us information when you contact us directly via email, for example, with support request or technical issue.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Log data: We collect information that mobile devices, and servers typically make available, such as IP address, unique device identifiers, language preference, the date and time of access, operating system, and mobile network information. We collect log information whenever you use our Services to monitor and improve our Services.
  • Usage data: We collect information about your usage of our Services. For example, we collect information about the actions that your perform in our mobile application such as which screens you visited or which users you interacted with. We use this information to provide our Services to you, as well as get insights on how people use our Services, so we can make our Services better.
  • Location data: We collect information about your precise location and we will ask you to allow us to do so through your mobile device operating system’s permissions. We only store your last known location. We use this information to enable you to see and interact with users around you.
  • Interactions with Other Users: We collect information about your interactions with other users while you are logged in to your account with us, such as exchanged "heys" and meet requests to provide our Services to you and to enable you to interact and meet with users in your area.
  • Stored data: We may access information stored on your mobile device via our mobile application. We access this stored information through your device operating system’s permissions. For example, if you give us permission to access the photographs on your mobile device’s camera roll, we may access the photos stored on your device when you upload a profile picture.
  • Mobile Device Access: We may request access or permission to certain features from your mobile device, including your mobile device's camera, and other features. If you wish to change our access or permissions, you may do so in your device's settings.
  • Push Notifications: We may request to send you push notifications regarding your account or the mobile application. If you wish to opt-out from receiving these types of communications, you may tum them off in your device's settings.
  • Information from Cookies & Other Technologies: A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Pixel tags (also called web beacons) are small blocks of code placed on websites and emails. We use cookies and other technologies like pixel tags to help us identify and track visitors, usage, access preferences for our Services and deliver targeted ads.
